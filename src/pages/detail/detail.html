2. Interface to customer about HW solution proposal and technical analysis.
3. Be responsible for the local development of hardware electronics, including customers requirement analysis/documents fill out, wiring harness solution, schematic design/simulation, BOM creation.

4. Knowledge of techniques for electrical design and lay-out aimed at EMC robustness of the product.
5. Experience in laboratory measurement and verification of characteristics of an electronic control circuit.
